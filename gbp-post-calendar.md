## Google's Content Policy Rules (Built Into Every Post)

These rules are non-negotiable. Violating any of them risks getting flagged or having posting suspended.

| Rule | Why |
|------|-----|
| **No medical/clinical claims about conditions** | Therapy practices can't describe what a condition *is* clinically (reserved for MDs/PhDs). Avoid: "It's a medical condition with..." Use: "If you're experiencing..." |
| **No treatment outcome claims** | Avoid: "heals best when met with..." Use: "therapy can support you through..." |
| **No phone numbers in post body** | Phone lives in the GBP profile only. Don't duplicate. |
| **No URLs in post body** | Links go in the CTA button only. Never paste URLs in the copy. |
| **No "free" offers in regular posts** | Use "complimentary" instead, or move to a GBP Offer post type. |
| **Stay informational, not promotional** | "Reach out" instead of "Book now!" |
| **Under 250 words per post** | Shorter is safer for AI scrutiny. |

## What to Avoid in Future Posts

These specific phrases got the May 4 version of the postpartum post flagged. Don't reuse them:

- "It's a medical condition with..."
- "...heals best when met with the right care"
- "specialize in maternal mental health and have advanced training in perinatal mood and anxiety disorders"
- Any clinical/diagnostic language about a condition
- Phone number anywhere in post body
- URL pasted in post body
- "Free" attached to a service offering

## Safer Alternatives

| Avoid | Use Instead |
|-------|-------------|
| "It's a medical condition" | "If you're experiencing..." |
| "Treat" / "treats" | "Support" / "work with" |
| "Heal" / "heals" | "Move through" / "process" |
| "Specialize in [diagnosis]" | "Focus on supporting women through..." |
| "Free 15-minute consultation" | "Complimentary 15-minute conversation" |
| Phone in body | Leave in profile field only |
| URL in body | Use CTA button only |
